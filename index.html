Our Story

Fashion That Doesn't Cost the Planet

2,400+Happy customers
6,000+Pieces rehomed
4.9★Avg. rating

Wheels Thrift started in a small Mumbai apartment with a single rack of pre-loved hoodies and a dream. We believed that great style shouldn't cost the earth — literally. Today we're a community of thrift lovers who believe fashion is most powerful when it's circular, intentional, and unapologetically personal.

Every piece you order from us has been sourced, cleaned, photographed, and priced by hands that genuinely care. No bots, no warehouses — just real people with a passion for vintage drip.
